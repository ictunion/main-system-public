tento email ti dochází jako členovi*ce výboru, protože přišla nová žádost o členství do Odborové organizace pracujících v ICT.

V příloze najdeš PDF přihlášku osoby, co žádá o členství.

  • Zájemci*kyni je třeba zavolat do dvou týdnů od obdržení tohoto emailu a zároveň před dalším výborovým setkáním, abychom o ní*něm mohli hlasovat.

🇬🇧 Hello,
you are receiving this email as a member of the committee, because a new membership application has been received for the Trade Union of Workers in ICT.

In the attachment you will find a PDF application from the person applying for membership.

What needs to be done now:

  • Check the generated application to ensure that no information is missing.
  • Write in the committee chat on Signal that a new application has been received and discuss who will call the applicant.
  • Call the applicant within two weeks of receiving this email and before the next committee meeting, so that we can vote on it.
  • You can find more information about the entire process in this manual (if you are a committee member, you should have access to it): https://docs.google.com/document/d/1pmkk1rnAZ5WVJEv_5Q0Yrw_XnEFSYoa9G-sU5N1Zzqg/edit?usp=share_link
